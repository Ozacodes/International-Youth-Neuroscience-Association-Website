What is the Neuro& Careers
Certificate Track?

The Neuro& Careers Certificate Track is an official certification program from the IYNA, designed to acknowledge your engagement in interdisciplinary neuroscience careers through the Neuro& Webinar Series.

Earn one of three certificates—Career Explorer, Career Navigator, or Career Pathfinder—by attending a specified number of webinars. Each certificate is a personalized digital document that serves as recognition of your commitment and participation.

To request your certificate, fill out the Certificate Request Form after meeting the eligibility requirements. The Neuro& team will verify your attendance and email the certificate directly to your inbox.

📋  The certificate is an official digital document signed by the Neuro& team and is valid upon reception. For full details, refer to the Information Document linked below.

How to Earn
Your Certificate

Follow these steps to receive your official IYNA certificate

1
Attend Neuro& Webinars

Participate in the Neuro& Webinar Series events. Your attendance will be tracked by the Neuro& team.

2
Meet the Eligibility Threshold

Reach the required webinar count for your desired certificate level (3, 6, or 12 webinars).

3
Submit the Certificate Request Form

Fill out the Certificate Request Form with your information. Ensure all details are accurate before submitting.

4
Attendance Verification

The Neuro& team reviews your submission and verifies your attendance records.

5
Receive Your Certificate

Your personalized, signed digital certificate will be emailed directly to your inbox upon verification.

Important Notes

✓

✓

✓

Certificates are sent to the email you provide — double-check your address before submitting.
